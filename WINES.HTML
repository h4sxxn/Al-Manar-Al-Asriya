Romanée-Conti Grand Cru

Price: £70,121

Romanée-Conti Grand Cru, crafted by the legendary Domaine de la Romanée-Conti in Burgundy, is the epitome of luxury and exclusivity in the wine world. With a limited annual production of only 5,000–6,000 bottles, it is a rare treasure highly coveted by connoisseurs. This wine is renowned for its exquisite balance, showcasing layers of red fruit, floral notes, and spices, with an unparalleled silky finish. Its vineyard, steeped in centuries of history, benefits from exceptional terroir and biodynamic practices. Offering Romanée-Conti elevates any occasion, embodying elegance and refined taste.

Corton Renardes, Grand Cru

Price: £48,619.22

Corton Renardes Grand Cru is a symbol of Burgundy's luxury and winemaking excellence, offering a bold and opulent experience in every sip. Crafted from premier Pinot Noir, it reveals layers of dark cherry, blackcurrant, and subtle spice, enriched by earthy, velvety undertones. This wine embodies sophistication, with its powerful structure and exceptional aging potential, making it a prized choice for collectors and connoisseurs. Sourced from the prestigious Corton hill, its terroir imparts unparalleled elegance and depth, making it a true masterpiece for those who seek rich, indulgent flavors.

Petrus, Pomerol, 2019

£18,009.61

Petrus 2019 from Pomerol is a masterpiece of Bordeaux winemaking, renowned for its unparalleled luxury and exclusivity. Crafted predominantly from Merlot, it boasts a rich and velvety texture with flavors of ripe plum, black cherry, and hints of truffle and cocoa, balanced by a perfect touch of minerality. This vintage is celebrated for its depth, complexity, and remarkable aging potential. Produced in limited quantities from an exceptional terroir of blue clay, Petrus 2019 is a symbol of sophistication and indulgence, offering a truly elite wine experience.

Château Lafite Rothschild, 1er Cru Classé, Pauillac, 1982

Price: £34,800.00

Château Lafite Rothschild 1982, a Premier Cru Classé from Pauillac, is one of the most iconic and luxurious Bordeaux wines ever produced. Celebrated for its extraordinary depth and elegance, this vintage offers a symphony of flavors, including blackcurrant, cedar, tobacco, and a hint of graphite, wrapped in velvety tannins. Renowned for its perfect balance and extraordinary aging potential, it represents the epitome of fine wine craftsmanship. With its legendary status and impeccable lineage, Château Lafite Rothschild 1982 is a true symbol of opulence and timeless sophistication.

Clos de la Roche, Vieilles Vignes, Grand Cru 2002

Price: £9,038.46

Clos de la Roche Vieilles Vignes Grand Cru 2002 is a pinnacle of Burgundian luxury, crafted from old-vine Pinot Noir that imparts exceptional concentration and complexity. This exquisite vintage unveils layers of dark cherry, blackberry, and plum, intertwined with earthy truffle, spice, and a touch of floral elegance. With its velvety texture and harmonious structure, it exemplifies the power and finesse of the Côte de Nuits terroir. A wine of remarkable depth and aging potential, it offers a luxurious experience for connoisseurs seeking the finest expressions of Burgundy.

Chevalier Montrachet, Grand Cru 1995

Price: £12,003.08

Chevalier-Montrachet Grand Cru 1995 is an extraordinary white Burgundy that epitomizes luxury and finesse. Crafted from Chardonnay grown in one of the most prestigious vineyards in the world, this vintage offers a harmonious blend of richness and elegance. It reveals layers of ripe pear, honeyed citrus, and toasted almond, complemented by delicate floral notes and a minerally backbone. With its silky texture, vibrant acidity, and remarkable aging potential, Chevalier-Montrachet 1995 is a true masterpiece, embodying the opulence and complexity that define great white wines of Burgundy.
